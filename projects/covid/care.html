Mask Care

SINGLE USE
(Medical and N95)
Medical masks are designed for one-time use. However, they are also in short supply and are highly pollutive to the environment.

For re-use, you can also give time for the virus to die before re-wearing, or sanitize the mask with liquid hydrogen peroxide. Do NOT use bleach, alcohol, sanitizing wipes, or soapy water.

Best strategy: if you have multiple masks, rotate use of the masks over several days.
MULTI USE
(All fabric masks)
