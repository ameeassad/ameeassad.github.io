Medical



Medical masks filter about 60 to 80 percent of particles. According to the Food and Drug Administration, it mostly blocks large-particle droplets, splashes, sprays or splatter that may contain germs.

When worn correctly, they can help prevent the spread of coronavirus by catching droplets when you cough or sneeze.




    MATERIALS:


  • PAPER-LIKE NONWOVEN MATERIAL

    TIPS

  • Make sure there are no gaps between the edge of the mask and your skin
