Fabric with Filter



Some fabric masks have a pocket sewn on the inside for you to add a filter for better protection. However, some filter materials present dangerous risks to your health: many are not breathable and may contain harmful fibers that you could inhale.




    FILTER MATERIALS:


  • Coffee filters: WARNING: using three or more coffee filters makes it difficult to breathe
  • Paper towels: A single paper towel filters 23 percent of 0.3 microns and two paper towels filtered 33 percent
  • Air filters: WARNING: when cut up, they can release fibers that can be dangerous to inhale, so the filter material should be sandwiched between layers of heavy cotton fabric
  • HEPA vacuum bags: WARNING: do not use if contains fiberglass

    TIPS

  • Whatever filter you use, make sure there is a layer of cotton or a similar material on either side of the filter, like the pocket in this mask
  • Make sure there are no gaps between the edge of the mask and your skin
